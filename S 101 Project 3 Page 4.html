Personal Anecdotes

During my time working as a laser engraver, I saw firsthand how access to technology can empower small businesses to offer personalized services at affordable prices. By utilizing cloud computing and digital tools, we were able to create customized products for clients worldwide, something that would have been impossible without today's technological advancements.

Another instance was when I taught my mother binary arithmetic. While initially unfamiliar, she quickly grasped how technology operates on basic principles. It became a great example of how accessible education can be when paired with technology.
